LET’S GET OUR SENIORS VACCINATED. REFER A SENIOR FOR VACCINATION AND BE REWARDED.

It is important that seniors get vaccinated against COVID-19 as they are the most vulnerable group and are at higher risk of becoming seriously ill, if infected. Taking the COVID-19 vaccine helps seniors to protect themselves and their loved ones.


Some seniors are hesitant about vaccination because they may not be familiar with the importance and benefits of vaccination or have concerns about receiving vaccination. Let’s encourage our seniors to be vaccinated and provide the support they need.

Refer our seniors for COVID-19 vaccination today.

For every successful referral of an unvaccinated senior aged 60 years and over, $30 HPB eVouchers will be given to the referrer as a token of appreciation once HPB has verified that the referred senior has completed both doses of the COVID-19 vaccination.

Important Notes:

Pre-registration for the programme will be closed on 23 November 2021. Pre-registration is mandatory criteria for interested referrers to be eligible for the token of appreciation.

Referrers should accompany the senior to the selected Vaccination Centres to receive his/her first dose of vaccination and complete registration for the programme on-ground by 30 November 2021.

All referrers who did not accompany seniors for their first dose at selected Vaccination Centres and complete registration for programme on-ground by 30 November 2021 will not be eligible.
